Classic Tiramisu

Prep time: 25 mins | Chill time: 4 hrs | Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 6 oz ladyfinger biscuits
  • 1 cup brewed espresso or strong coffee, cooled
  • 3 eggs, separated
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder (for dusting)

Instructions

  1. Beat egg yolks with sugar until pale and creamy.
  2. Fold in mascarpone until smooth.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip cream until stiff peaks form and fold into the mascarpone mixture.
  4. Dip ladyfingers briefly into coffee and layer in a dish.
  5. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  6. Repeat layers and finish with mascarpone on top.
  7. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  8. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.
