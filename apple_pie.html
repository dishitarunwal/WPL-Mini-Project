Apple Pie

Prep time: 20 mins | Cook time: 40 mins | Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup ice-cold water
  • 6 medium apples, peeled and sliced
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, butter, and water to make the pie dough. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  2. Combine sliced apples with sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, nutmeg, cornstarch, and salt.
  3. Roll out the dough and line a pie dish. Fill with apple mixture and cover with another layer of dough.
  4. Cut slits on top to let steam escape, then bake at 375°F for 40 minutes or until golden.
  5. Let cool and serve with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.
