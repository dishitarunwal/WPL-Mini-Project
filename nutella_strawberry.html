Nutella Strawberry Pancake

Prep time: 15 mins | Cook time: 5 mins | Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • Nutella
  • Fresh strawberries, sliced
  • Butter for cooking

Instructions

  1. Whisk together flour, egg, milk, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Heat a pan with butter and pour batter to form pancakes.
  3. Cook until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Spread Nutella on the pancakes, and top with fresh strawberries.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!
