Crepes

Prep time: 5 mins | Cook time: 10 mins | Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter for cooking

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, eggs, milk, melted butter, vanilla, and salt into a smooth batter.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan with butter. Pour in a small amount of batter.
  3. Cook for 1-2 minutes per side, flipping to brown both sides.
  4. Serve with your favorite fillings like fruits or Nutella.
