Macrons

Prep time: 30 mins | Cook time: 20 mins | Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 3 large egg whites
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cream of tartar
  • Buttercream or jam for filling

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 300°F (150°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Whisk egg whites with cream of tartar until soft peaks form, then add sugar.
  3. Fold in almond flour and powdered sugar mixture.
  4. Pipe the batter into circles on the prepared baking sheets.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes. Cool before filling with buttercream or jam.
