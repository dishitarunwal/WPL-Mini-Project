Creamy Mushroom Risotto

Prep time: 10 mins | Cook time: 25 mins | Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup white wine (optional)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, sauté onion and mushrooms.
  2. Add arborio rice, stir for 2 mins.
  3. Pour wine (optional), let it absorb.
  4. Add broth gradually, stirring constantly, until rice is creamy and cooked.
  5. Stir in butter and cheese. Serve warm.
