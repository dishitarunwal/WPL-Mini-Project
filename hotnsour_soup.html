Hot & Sour Soup

Prep time: 15 mins | Cook time: 20 mins | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp chili paste
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup tofu, cubed
  • 1/2 cup bamboo shoots
  • 1/2 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 cups water
  • Spring onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a pot, bring vegetable broth, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and chili paste to a boil.
  2. Add mushrooms, tofu, bamboo shoots, carrots, and ginger. Cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Mix cornstarch with water and add it to the soup to thicken it.
  4. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
