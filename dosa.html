Masala Dosa

Prep time: 20 mins | Cook time: 20 mins | Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dosa batter
  • 2 boiled potatoes, mashed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, onions, chili, turmeric. Sauté well.
  2. Add mashed potatoes and salt. Cook the filling.
  3. Pour dosa batter on hot tawa, spread thin.
  4. Place filling in center. Fold dosa and serve with chutney.
