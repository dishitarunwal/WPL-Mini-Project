Quiche Lorraine

Prep time: 15 mins | Cook time: 45 mins | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 pie crust
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup cooked bacon, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (Gruyère or Swiss)
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Whisk eggs, cream, milk, salt, and pepper together.
  3. Place bacon and cheese into the pie crust, then pour the egg mixture on top.
  4. Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until set and golden brown.
