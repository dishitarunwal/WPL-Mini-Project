Classic Margherita Pizza

Prep time: 20 mins | Cook time: 15 mins | Serves: 2-3

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Oregano and chili flakes to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Place the pizza base on a baking tray.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly on the base.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add tomato slices and fresh basil leaves.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle oregano/chili flakes.
  7. Bake in the oven for 12–15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Slice and serve hot!
