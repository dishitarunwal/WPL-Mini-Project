Pasta & Garlic Bread

Prep time: 15 mins | Cook time: 20 mins | Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (penne or spaghetti)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 tsp oregano & chili flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese (optional)
  • 4 slices French bread
  • 2 tbsp butter mixed with minced garlic & herbs

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and keep aside.
  2. Heat olive oil, sauté onions, bell peppers, and garlic until soft.
  3. Add tomato puree, salt, oregano, chili flakes and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Mix in the pasta and cook for another 2-3 minutes. Top with cheese.
  5. For garlic bread: spread garlic butter on bread slices and toast until golden.
  6. Serve hot pasta with crispy garlic bread on the side.
