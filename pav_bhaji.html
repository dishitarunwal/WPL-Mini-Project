Mumbai-Style Pav Bhaji

Prep time: 15 mins | Cook time: 25 mins | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 pav buns
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 capsicum, chopped
  • 2 tsp pav bhaji masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Lemon, coriander leaves

Instructions

  1. Heat butter and sauté onions till golden. Add tomatoes and capsicum and cook.
  2. Add mashed potatoes, peas, masala and mix well. Mash and simmer with water.
  3. Toast pav with butter on a tawa.
  4. Serve hot bhaji with buttered pav, lemon, and chopped onions.
