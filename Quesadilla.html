Cheesy Quesadilla

Prep time: 10 mins | Cook time: 10 mins | Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salsa or sour cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Place one tortilla in the pan and top with cheese, peppers, and onions.
  3. Cover with the second tortilla.
  4. Cook until golden brown on both sides and cheese melts.
  5. Slice and serve with salsa or sour cream.
