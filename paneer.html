Paneer Butter Masala

Prep time: 20 mins | Cook time: 25 mins | Serves: 3

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi

Instructions

  1. Sauté onions in butter until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, then tomato puree and spices. Cook well.
  3. Add cream and paneer. Simmer 5–7 minutes.
  4. Sprinkle kasuri methi and serve hot.

Veg Biryani

Prep time: 30 mins | Cook time: 30 mins | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans)
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • 1 tbsp biryani masala
  • Whole spices (cloves, bay leaf, cardamom)
  • 2 tbsp ghee/oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse and soak rice for 20 minutes. Cook 90% and set aside.
  2. Sauté whole spices and onion in ghee. Add veggies and cook.
  3. Add curd, biryani masala, salt. Layer with rice.
  4. Cover and cook on low flame for 15 minutes. Serve hot.
