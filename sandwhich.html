Masala Cheese Sandwich

Prep time: 10 mins | Cook time: 5 mins | Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bread
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves

Instructions

  1. Spread butter on one side of each bread slice.
  2. In a pan, sauté onions and tomatoes with spices until soft.
  3. Spread the mixture on one slice of bread and sprinkle cheese on top.
  4. Cover with the other slice and toast in a pan until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with coriander leaves.
