Spring Roll

Prep time: 20 mins | Cook time: 10 mins | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • Spring roll wrappers
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1/2 cup carrots, shredded
  • 1/4 cup bean sprouts
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix cabbage, carrots, bean sprouts, soy sauce, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Place the mixture in the spring roll wrappers and roll them tightly.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the rolls until golden brown.
  4. Serve with dipping sauce of your choice.
