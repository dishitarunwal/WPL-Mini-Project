Loaded Burrito

Prep time: 20 mins | Cook time: 15 mins | Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup black beans
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken or beef (optional)
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped lettuce
  • 2 tbsp sour cream or guacamole

Instructions

  1. Warm the tortillas slightly in a pan.
  2. Layer rice, beans, meat, salsa, cheese, and lettuce in the center of each tortilla.
  3. Add sour cream or guacamole.
  4. Fold in the sides and roll up tightly into a burrito shape.
  5. Optional: Grill the burrito for a crispy exterior.
