Noodles and Manchurian

Prep time: 20 mins | Cook time: 15 mins | Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup noodles
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. For Manchurian, mix vegetables, cornflour, and water to form a batter.
  3. Deep fry the batter in small portions to form Manchurian balls.
  4. In a pan, sauté the noodles with soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce, and cooked Manchurian balls.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your meal!
